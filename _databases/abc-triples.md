Lists of ABC triples (coprime a + b = c with radical(abc) < c) produced around the Dutch 'Reken mee met ABC' project and its ABC@home volunteer-computing network: the complete list of all 14,482,065 triples with c below 10^18 (goal reached in 2011) and 9,345,651 further triples with 10^18 <= c < 2^63 found in 2012-2015, both downloadable as gzipped text files. It also gives curated tables of the triples with c of at most 5 digits, the known 'good' triples of quality above 1.4, the highest-merit triples, and the 160 unbeaten triples.
