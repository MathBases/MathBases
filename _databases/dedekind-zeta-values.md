Numerical tables of the special values zeta_K(1-2i) at negative odd integers for the Riemann zeta function and for Dedekind zeta functions of totally real fields of small degree: real subfields of cyclotomic fields of orders 5, 7, 8, 11, 12 and 13, real quadratic fields of discriminant 5 through 44, cyclic cubic fields attached to 7th, 13th and 19th roots of unity, and four non-Galois cubic fields. Values were computed with PARI 2.0.17 at 180-digit working precision and are accompanied by prime factorizations of denominators.
