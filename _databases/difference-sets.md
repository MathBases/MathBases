A database of possible parameters for (v,k,lambda)-difference sets in abelian groups, listing all parameter sets with v < 100,000 that pass the basic nonexistence tests (counting, Schutzenberger, Bruck-Ryser-Chowla), with status for each (exists / all known / does not exist / open) and known difference sets included. Searchable by ranges of v, k, lambda and n, by group, and by status.
