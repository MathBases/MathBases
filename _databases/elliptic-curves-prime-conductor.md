Static tables of the 310,716 elliptic curves of prime conductor less than 10^8 found during 1989-90 computations at Fordham University, undertaken to explore the rank distribution of elliptic curves. The site provides downloadable curve files plus summaries, notation guides, talk slides, and the TeX/DVI source of the authors' 1990 Bulletin of the AMS paper based on the data.

This dataset is now subsumed by the LMFDB, whose elliptic curve database at https://www.lmfdb.org/EllipticCurve/Q/ includes all elliptic curves over Q of prime conductor up to 3·10^8.
