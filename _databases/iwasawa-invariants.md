Tables of the p-adic Iwasawa invariants mu, lambda, and Mordell-Weil lambda for elliptic curves over Q of conductor 1-5000 (in Cremona's labeling) at each prime p in {2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13}, computed with MAGMA. Supplementary tables cover quadratic twists of curves of conductor up to 100 and the curve 37A as p varies.
