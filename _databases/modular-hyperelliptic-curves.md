Summary of twelve tables of new modular hyperelliptic curves over Q of genus 2 to 6, split by genus and by whether the Jacobian is Q-simple. The genus-2 classification is complete; the higher-genus tables cover levels up to 3000 (Q-simple case) and 2000 (non-simple case). The tables accompany González-Jiménez-González (Math. Comp. 2003) and Baker-González-Jiménez-González-Poonen (Amer. J. Math. 2005).
