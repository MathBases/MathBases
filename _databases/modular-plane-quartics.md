A table of new modular non-hyperelliptic genus-3 curves over Q, i.e. smooth plane quartic models, with labels and defining equations. Entries are organized into three cases by the decomposition of the Jacobian: Q-simple, product of an elliptic curve and a Q-simple abelian surface, and product of three elliptic curves.
