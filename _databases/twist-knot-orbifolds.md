Companion data for two sections of Calegari-Dunfield's 'Automorphic forms and rational homology 3-spheres': Magma source code and data for the examination of congruence covers of twist-knot orbifolds, and a separate archive for the pro-p completions of the closed manifolds in the SnapPea census, each with a README documenting the format.
