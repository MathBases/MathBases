Interactive graphical polyhedra organized in over thirty categories, including Platonic, Kepler-Poinsot, Archimedean and Catalan solids, uniform star polyhedra and their duals, prisms and antiprisms, Johnson solids, geodesic polyhedra, toroids, higher-genus polyhedra and self-dual polyhedra. Each polyhedron's page provides a rotatable 3D model together with its vital statistics such as face, edge and vertex data.
